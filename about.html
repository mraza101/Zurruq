Our Story

From Ghanaian soil
to Australian tables.

Zurruq Origins was founded to bridge the gap between West Africa's exceptional agricultural heritage and Australia's demanding food industry. We saw a market flooded with untraceable commodity ingredients and knew there was a better way.

"We exist to make the finest West African cocoa and coffee as accessible and trustworthy as any locally produced ingredient."
The Source

Direct relationships. Unmatched quality.

Ghana is globally renowned for its premium cocoa, tightly regulated by the Ghana Cocoa Board (COCOBOD) to ensure consistent quality. We work exclusively with COCOBOD-licensed processors, ensuring our cocoa powder meets the highest international standards.

While Ghana's cocoa is famous, its coffee is a hidden gem. Grown by smallholder cooperatives in the Volta and Ashanti regions, Ghanaian coffee offers a unique, bright flavour profile that is severely underrepresented in the Australian specialty coffee scene. We are changing that by importing directly from the source.

Local Presence

Warehoused in Melbourne. Ready to dispatch.

The biggest challenge for Australian food manufacturers sourcing from Africa is supply chain uncertainty. We eliminate this risk entirely.

Our operations are based in Tullamarine, Victoria. We hold significant buffer stock locally, managing the complex logistics of sea freight, customs clearance, and quarantine so you don't have to. When you order from Zurruq Origins, your ingredients are dispatched from Melbourne within 48 hours.
