Supply Chain

Built for Australian supply chains.

We hold the stock so you don't have to. Fast dispatch, flexible MOQs, and reliable national freight from our Melbourne warehouse.

🏭

Melbourne Warehousing

Our primary distribution centre is located in Tullamarine, Victoria. This strategic location allows for rapid dispatch to major transport hubs and immediate access to the Port of Melbourne.

  • Temperature and humidity controlled
  • HACCP certified facility
  • Strict FIFO inventory management
  • Local pick-up available by appointment
⏱️

Dispatch Timelines

We understand that production schedules are tight. When you place an order with Zurruq Origins, we move quickly.

  • Orders processed within 24 hours
  • Standard dispatch within 48 hours
  • Same-day emergency dispatch available for contract clients
  • Full tracking provided upon dispatch
⚖️

Minimum Order Quantities

We cater to both large-scale manufacturers and independent food makers with flexible MOQs.

  • Cocoa Powder: 100kg (Wholesale) / 25kg (SMEs)
  • Green Coffee: 1x 60kg bag
  • Roasted Coffee: 10kg minimum
  • Pallet and container volumes available
🚚

National Freight

We partner with premium refrigerated and ambient freight providers to ensure your ingredients arrive in perfect condition.

  • Delivery to all Australian states and territories
  • Palletised freight for large orders
  • Courier delivery for SME orders
  • FOB Melbourne or FIS pricing available
Packaging

Packaging Specifications

Cocoa Powder

Packed in 25kg multi-wall kraft paper bags with a food-grade inner polyethylene liner to prevent moisture ingress and preserve flavour.

Pallet configuration: 40 bags (1000kg) per standard pallet, shrink-wrapped.

Green Coffee Beans

Packed in traditional 60kg jute bags lined with GrainPro hermetic bags to maintain optimal moisture content during transit and storage.

Pallet configuration: 10 bags (600kg) per standard pallet, shrink-wrapped.
