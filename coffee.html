Processing

Washed vs. Natural

Washed Process

The fruit is removed from the bean before drying. This results in a cleaner, brighter cup with pronounced acidity and clear flavour notes. Ideal for filter coffee or a bright espresso.

Natural Process

The bean is dried with the fruit still attached. This imparts a heavier body, intense sweetness, and complex fruity notes. Excellent for milk-based espresso drinks.
